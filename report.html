Project Report

The aim of this website is to promote my work and target potential clients across various industries. It showcases my expertise in Data Science, Machine Learning, and Cloud Solutions, offering a detailed view of my services, skills, and past projects to attract and engage potential clients.

1. Wireframes and Mockups

  • Comprehensive Diagrams: Wireframes and mockups covered all key pages, including Home, About, Services, and Contact. Each wireframe was crafted to match the visual style and layout of the final design.
  • Responsive Design Reflected: Diagrams showcased how elements would adjust across different screen sizes, demonstrating responsive design principles in action.
  • Professional Quality: Diagrams were clear, readable, and professionally presented, making them easy to translate into HTML and CSS. This approach ensured a seamless development process.

Images were generated using Canva with prompts such as "Generate an image for a consultant in Data Science and Machine Learning," capturing the professional essence of the site.

2. Accessible Design

The project prioritized accessibility to ensure the website is usable by as many people as possible, including those with disabilities. Specific accessibility features implemented include:

  • Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements are accessible via keyboard, allowing users with limited mobility to navigate the site easily.
  • ARIA Labels and Roles: Used across the site to improve screen reader experience, providing descriptive information about elements.
  • Contrast and Readability: Text and background color choices ensure high contrast ratios, meeting WCAG standards for readability.

3. Design and Planning

The design and planning phase was thoroughly executed, incorporating research and inspiration from industry-leading websites. Key areas of focus included:

  • Inspiration and Research: Reviewed modern web design trends to gather ideas that align with our project goals, ensuring a user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing design.
  • Usability Considerations: Layouts were crafted with user experience in mind, ensuring easy navigation and clear information hierarchy.
  • Learning and Adaptation: The planning process included iterative learning, allowing us to refine our design based on feedback and usability testing.

Micro images for user experiences and feedback sections were also generated using Canva, providing a consistent and engaging visual representation.

4. Reflection on Work

Throughout the development process, a critical reflection approach was maintained. Key reflections include:

  • Successes: The final product closely matched the initial mockups, showcasing a consistent design process from concept to execution.
  • Learning Outcomes: This project reinforced the importance of early planning, consistent testing, and the adaptability required in web design.

The reflection has provided valuable insights into the design process, highlighting areas of success and identifying opportunities for future improvements.

5. Background Music

The background music for the website was sourced from Artlist.io. The track titled "Good Feeling" by Jane & The Boy is licensed for free use, adding a dynamic and engaging atmosphere to the site.

6. Tools and Frameworks

The website was developed using the following tools and frameworks:

  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript: The core technologies used for building the structure, style, and interactivity of the site.
  • Swiper.js: Used for implementing the interactive sliders and testimonial sections, enhancing the user experience.
  • Canva: Used for generating professional-grade images and mockups that visually represent the content of the site.

These tools were chosen for their ability to streamline the design process and enhance the overall quality of the website.
